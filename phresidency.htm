What is a PH Permanent Residency?

PH Permanent Residency by Virtue of Marriage or by Investments allows eligible foreign nationals to live permanently or retire in the Philippines.

The 13A Marriage Visa allows foreign spouses of Filipino Nationals to live permanently in the Philippines. The main requirements for the 13a visa are:

  1. A Filipino spouse.
  2. A valid marriage certificate recognized by the Philippines.
  3. Sufficient financial means to support themselves and their family.
  4. The 13A is a multiple entry Non-Quota Immigrant Visa type, which means there is no annual limit on the number of visa applicants, and the visa allows for permanent residency. The Applicant can stay in the Philippines under a tourist visa and extend the status as applicable. This will allow the Applicant to stay in the Philippines indefinitely, provided the visa conditions are followed, i.e., a valid marriage with a Filipino citizen.

Process Timeline

  • 2-3 months from the time of submission of documents to BOI: Issuance of 13A Residence Card.

The Special Resident Retiree’s Visa (SRRV) is a special non-immigrant visa for foreign nationals who would like to make the Philippines their second home or investment destination. To qualify, the principal applicant must be;

  1. A foreign national or former Filipino citizen at least 50 years old
  2. Additional requirements may be requested depending on the SRR Visa Option to be chosen by the SRRV applicant.

Process Timeline

  • 2-3 months from the time of submission of documents to the PRA: Issuance of Card.
